About Us

This app is designed to help students practice and review multiple‑choice questions in a clear, reliable, and easy‑to‑use environment. By combining a simple interface with smart features like answer tracking and redo flows, it makes exam preparation more efficient and less stressful.

Our focus is on accessibility and trust. The app runs smoothly across platforms without requiring complex setup, and every detail — from the layout to the micro‑interactions — is built to feel polished and professional.

Please note: this app is an independent educational tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Cambridge Assessment or any official exam board. All content is provided for practice and learning purposes only.

This app has been developed independently by Nikhil Prashant Madathil.

Features

  • Answer Tracking: Review which questions you got right or wrong at a glance.
  • Redo Flow: Retry incorrect answers to reinforce learning.
  • Clean Interface: Simple design that keeps the focus on practice, not complexity.
  • Version Updates: Always up‑to‑date with the latest improvements and refinements.
